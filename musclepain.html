MUSCLE PAIN

Myofascial (Deep Muscle) pain is a syndrome categorized by chronic pain caused by multiple muscle trigger points and fascia tightening. The actual origin of pain is usually far from the location where a person experiences discomfort. Millions of people suffer from persistent tension in their head and neck muscles and they regularly rely on painkillers and ointments that only address the symptoms, not the root of the condition. Likewise, chiropractic and massage therapy also fail to provide permanent relief. Chronic Muscle pain may be generalized in nature and be accompanied with chronic fatigue. It may even be associated with Autoimmune disorders.


What are the symptoms of Myofascial Pain?

  • Headaches, jaw or facial pain

  • Pain that persists or worsens progressively
  • Weakness in the affected muscle and limited range of motion
