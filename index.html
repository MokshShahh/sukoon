ABOUT ME
I’m Dr. Raashi Khatri Panjabi
Dr. Raashi Khatri Panjabi is an MD with a specialization in Headaches, Chronic pain and Jaw joint Disorders. She pursued her Master’s degree at the prestigious Rutgers University, USA formerly known as the University of Medicine and Dentistry of New Jersey (UMDNJ) in Orofacial Pain and TMJ Disorders. She is a Diplomate of the American Board of Orofacial Pain (ABOP) and also has a Fellowship in Orofacial pain from NJDS-UMDNJ,USA. She has lectured extensively at prestigious national and international conferences and has had the opportunity to present her clinical research on "Pain and sensory changes in HIV+ patients".
